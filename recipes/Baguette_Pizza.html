Baguette Pizza

Description

Take a regular baguette and split lengthwise to create a tasty pizza. Toppings can be as simple as sauce and cheese, or upgrade with pepperoni and sausage.

Ingredients

  • 1 French Baguette
  • 1/2 Cup Pizza Sauce
  • 2/3 Cup Shredded Cheese
  • 2 Pepperoni Slices

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 375 Degrees
  2. Split Baguette in half lengthwise
  3. Spread Pizza Sauce on Baguette
  4. Bake in the Preheated Oven
