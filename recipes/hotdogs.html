Hot Dogs

Description

These air fryer hot dogs in toasted buns turn out perfectly crisp in minutes with the help of your air fryer. Top with ketchup, mustard, relish, chili, or condiments of choice

Ingredients

  • 4 Buns
  • 4 Hot Dogs

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 200 Degrees
  2. Place buns in a single layer in the air fryer basket
  3. Cook in the preheated air fryer until crisp, about 2 minutes
  4. Place hot dogs in a single layer in the air fryer basket; cook for 3 minutes
